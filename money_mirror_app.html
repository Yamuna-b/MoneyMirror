About & Help Guide

Understand the platform's scope, core metrics, and safety disclaimers.

📖 How it works & Core Metrics

Money Mirror utilizes a deterministic financial projection engine to simulate your cash flow over the selected horizon, accounting for interest growth, fixed costs, variable multipliers, and stress scenarios.

🛡️ Safety Runway

The number of months your savings are projected to last before falling below your specified Minimum Comfortable Balance if your income stops. Under 2 months indicates high risk, while 6+ months is highly secure.

🏦 Emergency Fund Ratio

Your savings divided by your total monthly expenses. This metric models how many months of standard living cost coverage you currently hold in liquid cash. Aim for at least 3.0×.

📊 Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio

The percentage of your monthly income spent on EMI liabilities. Calculated as Total EMIs / Total Income. Ratios exceeding 40% (Caution) or 50% (High Risk) require corrective action.

📈 Market Modes & Horizons

Adjusts interest rates and variable expense margins:
• Conservative: 4% annual interest + 10% variable buffer.
• Moderate: 8% annual interest.
• Aggressive: 12% annual interest - 5% variable buffer.

💡 A Concrete Example:

Suppose your profile inputs are:
• Take-Home Salary: ₹40,000 | Savings: ₹60,000 | Minimum Comfort Balance: ₹10,000
• Fixed Costs (Rent + Utilities): ₹10,000
• Discretionary / Variable Costs: ₹10,000
• EMIs: ₹12,000 (DTI ratio: 30%)
Results: Your total monthly expenses are ₹32,000, leaving a monthly cash surplus of ₹8,000. Your Emergency Fund Ratio is 1.88× (₹60,000 / ₹32,000). If your income stops, your savings will decline by ₹32,000/month; after 1 month, savings are ₹28,000, and in month 2 they dip below ₹10,000. Thus, your Safety Runway is ~1.5 months, placing you in a High Risk profile.

🔍 Platform Scope & Boundaries

Know what you can and cannot do inside the Money Mirror application.

✔️ What you CAN do:
  • Register an account securely.
  • Input income, EMIs, rent, expenses, and savings.
  • Plan active goals with custom target dates and amounts.
  • Test stress models using Conservative, Moderate, or Aggressive growth settings.
  • Examine metrics and save results to simulation history.
❌ What you CANNOT do:
  • Sync bank statements, credit card profiles, or read SMS.
  • Receive automated, regulatory financial/investment advice.
  • Run massive multi-tenant production scaling.
